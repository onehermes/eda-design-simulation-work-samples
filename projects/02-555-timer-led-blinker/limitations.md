# Limitations

- Simulation does not prove physical PCB reliability.
- LED current must be checked against the actual supply voltage and LED forward voltage.
- Power supply assumptions matter, especially startup behavior and loading.
- Component tolerances affect timing, duty cycle, and visual blink rate.
- Layout parasitics and assembly quality can change real-world behavior.

## Honest Scope Statement

This sample is intended to show structured EDA thinking and documentation quality. It is not evidence of manufactured hardware or lab-tested board performance.
